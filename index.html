Career Aspirations

My career goals are mainly oriented towards getting a job as a manager in technology. Being an individual who likes dynamism, I am attracted to the industries that use technology and the possibilities they present for leadership, innovation, and strategic choices. As far as I am concerned to be an effective manager, I have to get the following qualities: leadership, communication, strategic thinking, and technical proficiency.

Goals

According to industry insights and research, the key skills required for a management position in the technology sector include:

  • Leadership: Talent to motivate and inspire teams, achieve a good corporate culture and be the person others can follow.
  • Communication: Skilled with articulating ideas, strategies and objectives separately but in a coherent way to stakeholders at all levels.
  • Strategic Thinking: Ability to understand market trends, spot opportunities, and develop sustainable business plans for the goal of the company in a long term.
  • Technical Acumen: Understanding of emerging technologies, IT infrastructure, and industry-specific tools and platforms.
  • Problem-Solving: Ability to face challenges, find the best solution after analysing all the options and drive the organization to success.

I not only want to use the skills that I have earned in my educational life to achieve my career goals but also to contribute positively to the growth and the innovation of technology.
